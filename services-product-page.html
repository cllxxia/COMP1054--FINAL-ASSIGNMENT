FULL TUNE SERVICES

Our full tune services include base grinding, edge sharpening, and waxing to ensure your equipment performs at its best. This service covers:

  • Edge Sharpening
  • Base Grind
  • Hot wax and conditioning

For those looking for an extra level of performance, we also offer a stone grind option with our full tune service. This helps restore the base structure of your equipment, providing better glide and control.
