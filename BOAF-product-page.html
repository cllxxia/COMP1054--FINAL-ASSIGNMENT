BIRDS OF A FEATHER

The Birds Of a Feather is a women's specific technical adaptation of the best-selling D.O.A. series. It delivers the legendary performance of one of the world's most coveted snowboards, refined with flex patterns and geometric sizing to cater to women's specific riding preferences.

The Birds Of a Feather is a versatile, all-encompassing snowboard, ready to revolutionize your snowboarding experience and empower you to ride anywhere and conquer anything.

CAMBER

RESORT V1

Our RESORT V1 Profile board take full advantage of the benefits of traditional camber, zero camber, and reverse camber. This next-generation design allows you to get the pop and response of a cambered board with all the predictability and easy turn initation of zero camber or reverse camber shapes.
