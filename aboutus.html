HORIZON

PUSH THE LIMITS, BREAK THE BOUNDARIES

Welcome to Horizon! - Your Ultimate Destination for Snowboarding and Skateboarding! At Horizon, we're more than just a shop - we're a community of passionate riders who live for the thrill of the ride. Whether you're carving fresh powder on the slopes or cruising the streets, we've got you covered with the best gear, expert advice, and a whole lot of stoke.

Our Story: Born out of a love for boardsports, Horizon started as a local hangout spot for riders of all levels. We wanted a place where the community could connect, share stories, and gear up for their next adventure. Today, we're proud to be a trusted source for premium snowboarding and skateboarding, apparel, and accessories.

What We Offer

  • Top-tier snowboards, skateboards, and accesories from leading brands
  • Expert advice from riders who know their gear inside and out
  • A welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels at home
  • Community events, workshops, and meetups to keep the stoke alive

Join the Horizon Family: We're here to help you push your limits, express your style, and make unforgettable memories. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just getting started, Horizon is your go-to spot for all things skate and snow. See you on the slopes or in the streets - Keep shredding!
